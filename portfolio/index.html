About Me

"Unless you try to do something beyond what you have already mastered, you will never grow." - Ronald E. Osborn


Hi, my name is Sérgio and I am a 35 years old Portuguese Software Engineer who has been contracting in IT for over 10 years for major European Banks and Telco’s.

My development background so far has been mostly in Java, C++ and SQL but I am always on the lookout for the next interesting piece of software that needs to be built. The “how to build it”… that’s the FUN part!

I believe online education will play a major impact in our society in the coming years (and its long overdue!) with anyone having access to quality education regardless of their geographical location, wealth or social background.

I am also a great supporter of remote work who hates traffic and commuting every day to an Office just because I am “supposed to” when it’s far from being a productive environment.

Currently I am camper at FreeCodeCamp where I am learning new Web Development skills. Although I am currently not looking for a Web Developer job (the saying “never say never” just came to mind…) however learning new technologies and methodologies is really exiting, along with the possibility to help non-profits while doing it.

I would just like to say to the guys at FCC: thanks for allowing people like me to be a part of it.
